About Us

Jaxxe Studio is dedicated to transforming creative visions into reality through innovative design and multimedia solutions.

  • 2021-2022

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Jaxxe Studio started from humble beginnings, where a small team of passionate creators came together in a modest workspace to turn their shared vision into reality. With limited resources but an abundance of creativity and determination, we focused on delivering innovative design and multimedia solutions. Our journey has been marked by challenges and learning experiences, but each step has strengthened our commitment to excellence and our desire to grow as a studio. Today, we celebrate our evolution while remaining grounded in the values that shaped our foundation.

  • March 2022

    An Agency is Born

    Jaxxe Studio was born in Padang, Indonesia, from a vision to create a vibrant hub for creativity and innovation in the local arts scene. Founded by a group of passionate individuals who shared a love for design and multimedia, the studio emerged as a response to the growing demand for high-quality creative services in the region. With a commitment to excellence and collaboration, Jaxxe Studio quickly established itself as a go-to destination for businesses and individuals seeking unique branding solutions, captivating visual content, and engaging marketing strategies. The studio's journey reflects its dedication to nurturing local talent while contributing to the cultural landscape of Padang.

  • December 2022

    Transition to Full Service

    The transition to a full-service agency marks a significant evolution for Jaxxe Studio, allowing us to offer a comprehensive suite of creative solutions tailored to our clients' diverse needs. Initially focused on specific design services, we expanded our capabilities by bringing in experts across various disciplines, enabling us to deliver cohesive strategies that align with client objectives. This client-centric approach allows us to manage projects from inception to execution, fostering long-term partnerships and streamlining the creative process as we navigate the ever-evolving landscape of digital marketing and brand development together.

  • July 2024

    Phase Two Expansion

    The Phase Two expansion of Jaxxe Studio represents a pivotal moment in our growth, aimed at enhancing our service offerings and capabilities. This expansion will allow us to incorporate advanced technologies and tools, enabling us to deliver even more innovative and comprehensive creative solutions. By investing in new talent and resources, we aim to broaden our reach and strengthen our position as a leading agency in the creative industry. This phase not only reflects our commitment to excellence but also our dedication to meeting the evolving needs of our clients, ensuring that we remain at the forefront of design and multimedia production.

Branding brand for Coca Cola

The brand is synonymous with happiness, sharing, and moments of joy, often seen in its iconic red and white branding and memorable advertising campaigns. Coca-Cola’s commitment to sustainability and community-driven initiatives further solidifies its position as a leader in the beverage industry.

Southwest

Website Design for Southwest

*Web design* is the art and practice of creating visually appealing, user-friendly websites that effectively communicate a brand’s message and provide a seamless experience for visitors. It involves a combination of layout design, color schemes, typography, and interactive elements to ensure a website is both aesthetically pleasing and functional across all devices. A well-designed website not only looks good but also prioritizes usability, speed, and accessibility, making it easy for users to navigate and find the information they need. Whether for a business, personal blog, or e-commerce platform, web design plays a crucial role in engaging users, building trust, and driving conversions.

*Photography* is the art and technique of capturing moments, scenes, and emotions through the lens of a camera.
